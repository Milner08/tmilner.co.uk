About Me

Hello! I'm a software developer from the UK. You can normally find me traveling, working or taking photos. I've been working at IBM since graduating from Swansea University in 2015.

Whilst at Uni I completed several projects, including a social app for country walks and rambling (with REST api, website and Android app). One of my favourites was using Fuzzy Logic control to balance a Lego robot. Spoiler - It didn't work very well, but I learnt a lot.

So far the majority of my professional experience has been with Java and C, working on the RAID and Cache layers of IBM Spectrum Virtualize.

When not at work I like to experiment with web development and newer technologies such as Go, Node.JS and Angular.JS.

White Rock Walking Trails

During my masters course I had to complete a group industry project. The result of this is the White Rock Trails app and website. It was created for the White Rock history society in Swansea bay. We held regular meetings with a representative from the society and worked to create something that met their requirements. The final app had the ability to design a walk and to follow an existing walk. Along the route information about the area could be displayed including historical accounts and photographs.

  • Client: White Rock Trails, Swansea
  • Date: 2014
  • Service: Web Development, REST API Development, Backend Development, Android App Development, Project Planning.
